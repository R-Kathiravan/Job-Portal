Engagement Models

Choose the partnership model that fits your hiring needs.

Contingency Search

No win, no fee

The client only pays a percentage fee if they successfully hire a candidate Stackly provides. Great for mid-level roles and competitive hiring scenarios.

  • Payment only on success
  • No upfront costs
  • Risk-free commitment
  • Ideal for mid-level hires

Retained Search

Dedicated, priority headhunting

The client pays an upfront retainer for Stackly to drop everything and exclusively hunt for a highly specialized or executive role.

  • Exclusive dedicated resources
  • Priority scheduling
  • Proactive candidate outreach
  • Perfect for executive roles
