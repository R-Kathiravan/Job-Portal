Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How quickly can you provide candidates?

Our average time-to-fill is 14 days. For urgent requirements, we can provide an initial shortlist within 24-48 hours thanks to our extensive network of 50,000+ pre-vetted professionals.

What is your success fee structure?

We offer flexible engagement models including contingency search (no upfront cost, pay only on success) and retained search (dedicated resources with priority scheduling). Contact us for a customized quote based on your needs.

Do you offer any guarantees?

Yes! We offer a 90-day replacement guarantee. If our placed candidate isn't the perfect fit within the first 90 days, we will replace them at zero additional cost.

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize in technical talent across all major tech stacks including Frontend (Angular, React, Vue), Backend (.NET, Node.js, Python), Cloud (AWS, Azure, GCP), Mobile, DevOps, and AI/ML domains.

How do you vet your candidates?

Our rigorous 7-step verification process includes technical assessments, behavioral interviews, reference checks, background verification, skills testing, culture fit assessment, and credential validation.

Can you help with remote hiring?

Absolutely! We have experience placing remote talent globally and can help you build distributed teams. We understand the unique challenges of remote hiring and can source candidates comfortable with remote work arrangements.
